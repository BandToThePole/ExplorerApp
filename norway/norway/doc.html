Connection

This app supports connecting to multiple Microsoft Bands, although only a single Band can be used at once. After firstly ensuring that the Band is paired with your iPhone you will then see the Band listed on the Connection tab. Tap on a Band to connect to it. Once a Band has been connected to a checkmark will show next to its name.

The app will automatically connect to the most recently connected to Band when it is first launched.

Recording

After a connection to a Band has been established you can begin a recording session, which is a short period for which sensor data is collected from the Band and stored on your iPhone - it won't yet be transmitted anywhere. It is important to ensure that you manually stop a recording session within the app before you attempt to sync sessions.

Sessions

All recorded sessions data can be accessed from within the app, with visual indicators to show whether the data has been synced to the cloud.

Sending

Firstly, you will need an account on the Band to the Pole web server. When you first login it will verify that your account details are correct, so it is important to have an Internet connection when you first do this. There are three sync options:

  • Email: all recorded session data is compressed and attached to an email.
  • Sync new: attempts to sync all previously unsynced sessions. If the sync doesn't succeed the sessions won't be marked as synced, and they can be synced in the future instead.
  • Sync all: does the same as 'Sync new', but sends all data (the server handles duplicates).

The data from a single recording session will generally be on the order of a few hundred bytes, which is way under the size of a single TCP/IP packet.
